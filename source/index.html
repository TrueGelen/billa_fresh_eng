Freshness
in content

or focus on Billa values

Tasks
of the client

  • Conceptually change and improve the content quality on Instagram,
  • Develop a new content strategy,
  • Focus the audience on company values: freshness and quality.

The target
audience

  • Women and men, 25+
  • Income: below average/average/above average
  • Average household size: 3 people
  • Interests: cooking, fresh and high-quality products, family and family leisure.

What decision
did we offer

Freshness is one of the main characteristics of product quality. This is what every customer pays attention to and what primarily affects their choice. The decision based on this fact was to develop a new content strategy with an emphasis on company values ​​so that users have no doubts about the quality guarantee of Billa!

Mandatory content elements of the new strategy are fruits and vegetables. We proposed the use of an endless feed in the Billa Instagram account to create a complete sense of freshness. In addition to the already well-known humanization of products, the replacement of recognizable objects and forms with fresh fruits and vegetables is also used.

Why did we
choose
particular
this decision

In our opinion, the content should reflect the main values of the company, and that is why we decided to completely change the concept of Instagram and introduce the Billa brand from its best!
